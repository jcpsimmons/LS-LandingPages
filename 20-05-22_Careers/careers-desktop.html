Good Vibes

We are a team built on positive energy, infinite enthusiasm and constant encouragement. Every department works together to make every guest experience better, because we believe that if we’re having a good time, our guests will too.

Great Benefits

Every full-time Living Spaces team member has access to our competitive benefits package, which includes medical, dental and vision insurance, as well as a 401K program. Team members also have paid vacation and sick days.

Growth Opportunities

From day one, we set in motion a plan for continued development and success. As Living Spaces grows, so will you. Also, thanks to our promote-from-within policies, we ensure you have every opportunity to take your career to the next level.

Retail Careers

Our retail team members guide and educate guests as they discover the styles and designs they want to bring home. Building a relationship based on trust is essential to helping our guests find the perfect pieces, and we are proud to be a part of transforming and elevating their living space.

Corporate Careers

Our corporate team members encompass a wide variety of departments and talents that are crucial to all that happens behind the scenes. From merchandising, marketing and training, to IT, accounting and human resources, these team members are the backbone of our business.

Guest Services Careers

Our guest services team members work hard to address and resolve any issues guests may have throughout the shopping process. Whether they communicate via phone calls, web chats or emails, these team members provide excellent service for every guest, during every communication, every day.

Distribution Center Careers

Distribution and logistics team members support the end-to-end distribution and delivery structure at Living Spaces. All departments, including receiving, shipping and quality control, help ensure we provide a level of service that surpasses our guests’ expectations.

Manufacturing

Our manufacturing team members find satisfaction in creating items with their hands and get to experience a sense of accomplishment with every piece they create. From seamstresses, to upholsterers, to cushion fillers, all team members work together to make items guests will love.

Mission: Fast, Fun + Easy Shopping

Finding the right furniture can be overwhelming, but we make it fast, fun and easy for everybody no matter what their budget. By shopping with us in store or online and following our Essential Shopping Checklist, guests can buy their pieces today, enjoy them tonight and celebrate life's moments in a home they love.
