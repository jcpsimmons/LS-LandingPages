Giving Spaces

Our mission is to help everyone create a better home. A home where you can find support, security, comfort and beauty. We host giving campaigns and partner with foundations that have a similar purpose, so we can expand our efforts to a variety of communities in need, from veterans and foster care children, to victims of recent disasters, including Hurricane Harvey and the California fires.

Because one of the most vital essentials is a safe place to sleep, we have set up ongoing mattress donations through our Revive Sleep Centers, and we extend giving opportunities to our guests through special, continual events.

Learn more about our work with the community below. For additional information, please email us at community@livingspaces.com.
