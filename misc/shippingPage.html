Full-Service Delivery FAQ

Does Living Spaces offer Same Day and Next Day delivery?

Yes, Full-Service Delivery offers Same Day and Next Day options. To determine eligibility, please view your delivery options here.

Is there a minimum purchase required to qualify for Full-Service Delivery?

No minimum purchase is required, but additional fees may apply.

Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a delivery confirmation email from Living Spaces. When you receive the delivery confirmation email, please allow up to 48 hours for tracking information to be updated on the delivery carrier's website. We recommend tracking your order to ensure it is delivered successfully.

Does Living Spaces deliver to all 50 States and P.O. Boxes?

Living Spaces delivers to the lower 48 states but does not deliver to P.O. Boxes. Same Day and Next Day options are not available in all markets. To determine eligibility, please view your delivery options here.

What are the exchange/return fees associated with Full-Service Delivery items?

Returns on regular stock items will require a 10% restock fee plus a pickup fee of $99-$139 depending on day of pickup, if pickup is needed.

Exchanges on regular stock items for greater or equal value require a 5% restock fee plus an additional delivery fee. Exchanges on regular stock items for lesser value require a 10% restock fee plus an additional delivery fee.

For more information on exchanges and returns, please click here.

Shipping FAQ

What is the minimum purchase subtotal needed to qualify for Free Shipping?

The minimum purchase subtotal needed to qualify for free shipping is $500.00. Taxes and Care Free fees are not included in the subtotal.

Do Clearance or Accessories items qualify for Free Shipping?

Accessories and Clearance items are accounted for to meet the $500.00 minimum purchase subtotal to qualify for free shipping. All accessories qualify for free parcel shipping.

May I purchase a Care Free Plan to reach the $500.00 order minimum to be eligible for Free Shipping?

No, the $500.00 minimum applies to product only. It does not include the Care Free cost or sales tax.

What is the shipping fee for orders less than the $500.00?

Shipping fees start at $59 or free with in-store pickup. Shipping fees may vary for regions outside of our standard service areas. Regions outside of our service area may also be ineligible for Free Shipping.

How soon can I expect my items under the Shipping plan?

Items sent via Free Shipping can be scheduled as early as 2 days from purchase. Check your estimated delivery time here.

When can I expect my shipment?

Shipping deliveries are completed Monday-Sunday. Shipments outside of our standard service areas are delivered Monday-Saturday. A 3-hour window will be provided the night before shipment for all standard service areas.

Must I be present during the time of delivery?

Yes, a person 18 years or older is required to be home and sign to accept the shipment.

Where will my furniture be placed, upon delivery, under the Shipping plan?

For single family homes, the placement of items will be inside of the front door or garage

For apartment buildings with a lobby, the placement of items will be in the lobby.

For apartment building without a lobby, the placement of items will be inside the apartment’s front door.

Does the Shipping plan include unpacking, unwrapping, assembly, or the removal of packaging?

No.

Does the Shipping plan include pickup and removal of my old mattress?

No. Only mattress orders with Full Service Delivery include removal of old mattress.

What are the exchange/return fees on items delivered via Shipping?

Returns on regular stock items will require a 10% restock fee plus a pickup fee of $59, if pickup is needed or if customer was provided free shipping.

Exchanges on regular stock items for greater or equal value require a 5% restock fee plus an additional delivery fee. Exchanges on regular stock items for lesser value require a 10% restock fee plus an additional delivery fee.

For more information on exchanges and returns, please click here.

What are the customer packaging requirements for exchanges and returns?

Products delivered via shipping (not Full Service Delivery) are required to be placed back into the packaging in which they arrived for all exchanges and returns. Products not returned to their original packaging will not be accepted.

Once a delivery truck has been dispatched, will I be charged for any Not at Home, Reschedule or Missed Delivery situations? If so, what amount?

Yes, there will be a fee if you are not present at the time of delivery or do not reschedule 48 hours before your delivery date. The fee for a rescheduled delivery is $59.

May I change my delivery method from Shipping to Full Service?

Yes, you may change your delivery method by clicking here. Please note that if your delivery date is less than 48 hours away, rescheduled delivery will not be possible.

I lost my assembly instructions; can Living Spaces send me another copy?

Product assembly instructions are available on our Living Spaces website. Select the product for which you require instructions. Within the webpage for the selected product, assembly instructions can be found by scrolling down to the bottom of the “Product Details” section. If the product is no longer showing online. Our Guest Services team will be happy to send you a copy. You can quickly contact Guest Services via chat by clicking here.

Parcel Shipping FAQ

When are items delivered via Parcel Shipping?

If you purchased any rugs, home décor, and/or Bed-in the-Box mattresses, the items will ship via UPS (Ground or Freight). A separate tracking number will be provided by UPS, but you may track your entire order using the Living Spaces’ tracking tool.

May I request Parcel Shipping?

No need to request it. Items available for Parcel Shipping will automatically be setup for Parcel Shipping when you checkout.

Is Parcel Shipping free?

Yes. Enjoy free Parcel Shipping nationwide for all our rugs, pillows, wall art, and other home accessories. No minimum amount is required.
