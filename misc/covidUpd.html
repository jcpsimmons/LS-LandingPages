Safe Shopping During COVID-19

The health and wellbeing of our guests and employees is our top priority, and maintaining your trust is a responsibility we take seriously. Here are the steps we’re taking to create a safer shopping experience. To maintain social distancing, we are limiting the number of guests shopping at a time.

What You Can Expect at Our Stores

Face masks required

All guests are required to bring and wear their own face masks.

Temperature checks

Guests will undergo temperature checks prior to entering the showroom.

Cash or checks NOT accepted at this time

Apple Pay or Google Pay is preferred.

Social distancing

We ask that guests keep a minimum of six feet apart inside our stores.

Supervised Kids’ Room closed

Our Kids’ Fun Room will remain closed at this time.

Thorough safety measures

  • Increased sanitization protocols with all surfaces wiped down multiple times each day
  • Stylus pens for no contact transactions
  • Protective sheets available
  • Hand sanitizer available
